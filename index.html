About me


I am a former employee of Liverpool FC where I was part of the partnership insight team for over 3 years. Before taking the position I was a part-time sales advisor at the Liverpool FC store while completing my post-graduate certificate in Football Industries at the University of Liverpool. I also have an MBA from Liverpool John Moores University where I gained knowledge around business management, operations, strategy and talent recruitment. Apart from all of that I have been trying to work on a few personal projects in order to gain some knowledge in front-end development using HTML and CSS, as well as some database and back-end development knowledge with MySQL and Python. I have also worked in the gaming industry for a year in 2012 as a new business consultant for a company based in Gibraltar.

In my free time I generate analytical reports on Football players and teams, having also done freelance scouting for the Professional Football Scouts Association across League 2 and Conference divisions in the UK. Ultimately my objective is to combine both my passions for football and data in my next role, working in performance analysis and recruitment. As my most recent and longest role was in researching potential companies, my strengths are in research, writing reports, using data to make informed decisions.

It is also important for me to pass on my knowledge and experience to the next generation of students, especially those interested in pursuing a career in Football, and I have had the chance to do that through some speaking opportunities. About this website


Having left my recent job, I spent some time getting into the world of tech and travelling. During my time in Michigan, I learned HTML and CSS because in addition to having a good LinkedIn profile and CV, I wanted to showcase my qualifications and tech skills by also having a personal website to increase my chances of finding my next home. It's been a mini-project that I took on for a month or so, insiting on developing it from scratch and not using any pre-designed web templates.

Throughout the years and until today I have been in contact with organizations such as UEFA, Adidas, Arsenal FC, Everton FC, Manchester City, SEGA, Formula E, BetVictor, Liverpool FC with the hope that my next destination is at a brand of similar magnitude. For this purpose I have also invested a lot of time in travelling and discovering interesting spots in the world that I have shared in my gallery section.
